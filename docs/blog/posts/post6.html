Works Offline — Forever

Works Offline — Forever

Published: November 27, 2025

One of the most powerful features of AxelBase is simple: once you’ve opened it once, it works forever — with or without internet.

How True Offline Mode Works

AxelBase is a 100% static SvelteKit app. After the first visit:

  • All JavaScript, CSS, and HTML are cached
  • Your browser can serve the entire tool from disk
  • No network requests are made on reload

Real Offline Use Cases

  • Verify files on an air-gapped workstation
  • Check hashes during travel (plane, train, ship)
  • Audit files in secure facilities
  • Use in areas with poor connectivity

How to Make It Permanent

Click “Add to Home Screen” (mobile) or “Install” (desktop Chrome/Edge) → it becomes a standalone app with its own window and icon.

Or simply bookmark it and disable internet — it will still work perfectly.

Your tools should work anywhere. AxelBase does.
