Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 22, 2025

Welcome to AxelBase File Hash Calculator — a completely client-side web tool that computes SHA-256 and MD5 hashes directly in your browser. We take your privacy extremely seriously, which is why we designed this tool with one core principle: your files never leave your device.

We Collect Absolutely Nothing

Because all hashing happens locally using the Web Crypto API:

  • No files are uploaded — ever
  • No IP addresses are logged
  • No usage analytics — we don’t use Google Analytics, Plausible, or any tracking
  • No cookies, localStorage, or fingerprints
  • No server-side processing at all

When you close the tab, everything disappears. We have no way — and no desire — to see what files you hash.

How It Actually Works

Your browser reads your file in secure chunks, feeds them directly into the built-in cryptographic engine, and displays the result. Nothing is sent over the internet. Even if you're completely offline after the first load, the tool works perfectly.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any external analytics, advertising, or CDN-tracked scripts. The only external resource is an optional favicon. All code runs from your device or trusted static hosting (GitHub Pages, Vercel, etc.) with no telemetry.

Your Responsibility

While we guarantee no data leaves your browser, you remain responsible for:

  • Using the tool on a secure, updated device and browser
  • Not sharing screenshots containing sensitive hashes
  • Verifying you're on the official domain

Children’s Privacy

This tool is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if we add new features. You’ll always find the latest version here.

Your privacy is not a feature — it’s the entire product. Thank you for trusting AxelBase.
